CanSat

Altitude's satellite is built around an Arduino Uno R3 controller and a repeatable 3D-printing workflow using Bambu Lab hardware.

Controller Arduino Uno R3 Microcontroller
Fabrication Bambu Lab X2D Printer Combo, AMS 2 Pro, and two AMS HT dryers
Filament ASA Aero, TPU 90A, and PETG CF

Mission Hardware

The devices below support the two sides of the payload: embedded control and a controlled fabrication workflow.

Arduino Uno R3

The Uno R3 is the CanSat's microcontroller platform. It is used to run mission code, coordinate sensor readings, manage timing, and provide a clear, testable control layer for the payload.

Bambu Lab X2D Printer Combo

The X2D printer combo is used with the AMS hardware to manufacture the CanSat structure, fixtures, test pieces, and revised CAD iterations. Fast printing helps the team test fit and strength before finalising a design.

AMS Fabrication System

The AMS 2 Pro and two AMS HT dryers are part of the fabrication process. They manage multi-material printing and keep engineering filaments dry for consistent structural parts.

Filaments ASA Aero, TPU 90A, and PETG CF give the team options for lightweight structure, flexible parts, and carbon-fibre-reinforced prints.
CAD Lets the team tune clearances, board mounts, wall thickness, and parachute or recovery interfaces before printing.
Tests Every printed iteration can be handled, checked, revised, and documented before the final competition build.

Build Workflow

The project moves from model to print to electronics integration, with notebook evidence collected along the way.

1

Design

CAD defines the CanSat body, internal mounts, access points, and recovery interfaces.

2

Print

The X2D, AMS 2 Pro, and AMS HT dryers produce prototypes and final parts with controlled ASA Aero, TPU 90A, and PETG CF handling.

3

Integrate

The Arduino platform and payload electronics are fitted, wired, and checked against the physical layout.

4

Document

Testing, failures, changes, and final decisions are recorded in the engineering notebook.
