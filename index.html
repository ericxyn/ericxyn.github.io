The CanSat Competition

CanSat challenges teams to design a working satellite system small enough to fit inside a drinks-can-sized volume.

Altitude's job is to turn a compact payload into a complete engineering project: structure, electronics, software, testing, documentation, and a mission that can survive launch and descent.

The competition rewards teams that can combine reliable hardware with clear decision-making. That means every design choice needs evidence, from print materials and mounting points to sensor logging and recovery workflow.

4 Team members covering leadership, code, CAD, build, and documentation.
1 Integrated CanSat system built around a compact Arduino-controlled payload.
