Team

Four focused roles, one shared mission: build a reliable CanSat and explain every engineering decision clearly.

Members Eric, Lewis, Sebastian, Kamran
Focus Code, CAD, build, leadership, notebook

Eric - Code | CAD

Eric works across the flight software and the mechanical model, linking the code architecture to the real shape of the payload. His role keeps the electronics, mounting geometry, and mission logic moving together.

  • Flight logic
  • Data handling
  • CAD integration

Lewis - Team Leader

Lewis coordinates the team, tracks deadlines, and keeps decisions connected to the competition brief. His leadership role turns separate workstreams into one coherent engineering project.

  • Planning
  • Coordination
  • Mission decisions

Sebastian - Build

Sebastian focuses on turning designs into physical hardware. He handles assembly, fit checks, structural adjustments, and the practical testing that shows whether the payload can survive handling and launch conditions.

  • Assembly
  • Fit testing
  • Build quality

Kamran - Notebook | CAD

Kamran documents the project while contributing to the CAD workflow. His notebook work records the reasoning behind tests, failures, material choices, and design changes so the team can defend the final satellite.

  • Engineering notebook
  • CAD support
  • Design evidence
